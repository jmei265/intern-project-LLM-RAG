Outlook forms are used as templates for presentation and functionality in Outlook messages. Custom Outlook forms can be created that will execute code when a specifically crafted email is sent by an adversary utilizing the same custom Outlook form.[1]Once malicious forms have been added to the user’s mailbox, they will be loaded when Outlook is started.
