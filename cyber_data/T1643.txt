This is typically performed to manipulate external outcomes, such as to achieve carrier billing fraud or to manipulate app store rankings or ratings. Outbound traffic is typically generated as SMS messages or general web traffic, but may take other forms as well.If done via SMS messages, Android apps must hold theSEND_SMSpermission. Additionally, sending an SMS message requires user consent if the recipient is a premium number.
