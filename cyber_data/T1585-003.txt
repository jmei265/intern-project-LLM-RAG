Adversaries can use cloud accounts to further their operations, including leveraging cloud storage services such as Dropbox, MEGA, Microsoft OneDrive, or AWS S3 buckets forExfiltration to Cloud Storageor toUpload Tools. Cloud accounts can also be used in the acquisition of infrastructure, such asVirtual Private Servers orServerlessinfrastructure.
