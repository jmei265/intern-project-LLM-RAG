Supply chain compromise of software can take place in a number of ways, including manipulation of the application source code, manipulation of the update/distribution mechanism for that software, or replacing compiled releases with a modified version.Targeting may be specific to a desired victim set or may be distributed to a broad set of consumers but only move on to additional tactics on
